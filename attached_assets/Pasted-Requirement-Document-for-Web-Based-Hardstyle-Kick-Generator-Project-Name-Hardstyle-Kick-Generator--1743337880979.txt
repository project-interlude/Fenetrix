1. Introduction
The Hardstyle Kick Generator (HKG) is a web-based tool that enables producers to generate high-quality hardstyle kicks with customizable punch, crunch, and key selection. It provides different distortion styles to achieve various hardstyle kick types, such as gated rawstyle kicks, euphoric kicks, and zaag kicks. Users can tweak parameters to shape the kick, apply post-processing effects, and download the final result as an audio file.

2. Key Features
2.1 Kick Generation
✅ Generate hardstyle kicks from scratch.
✅ Support for key selection from C0 to C5 for tuning flexibility.
✅ Two main layers:

Punch Layer (Transient, click, and attack characteristics).

Crunch Layer (Body, tail, and distortion processing).

2.2 Distortion & Processing Effects
✅ Different distortion types for various kick styles:

Gated Rawstyle Kicks – Aggressive, rhythmic gated distortion.

Euphoric Kicks – Warm and round distortion with a smooth tail.

Zaag Kicks – Overdriven, saw-like distorted tail.

Classic Reverse Bass Kicks – Fast decay, sub-heavy bass.

✅ Additional sound-shaping effects:

EQ (pre/post distortion) for frequency sculpting.

Saturation for analog-style warmth.

Reverb & Delay for space and depth.

Bitcrushing & Downsampling for lo-fi textures.

Phase control & layering options to stack kick components.

2.3 User Customization & Controls
✅ Kick Shaping Parameters:

Pitch envelope control (adjust the punch and tail movement).

Attack, decay, sustain, release (ADSR) settings.

Click noise and transient shaping.

Hardness & distortion intensity.

Sub-bass amount (control for deep low-end).

✅ Real-Time Preview & Visualization:

Waveform display for visual feedback.

Frequency spectrum analyzer.

A/B comparison of processed vs. unprocessed kick.

✅ Preset System:

Default kick presets for quick start.

Save/load user-defined presets.

✅ Export Options:

Download in WAV (24-bit, 44.1kHz/48kHz).

Option to render with or without effects.
